Trial of Two

Two players must collaborate from different computers to try and solve a puzzle. The players control a character from a top down point of view, moving objects, hitting switches and leaping over chasms to solve the puzzle.

A Pandacodium 2013 project, it was created in 48 hours by just two people using HTML5 and web sockets.
